Welcome to Cryptic Penguin Tech

We specialize in building custom computers for gaming, business, office, and school use. Our computers are tailored to meet your specific needs and budget.

About Us

Cryptic Penguin Tech is a small town local business dedicated to providing high-quality computer solutions to our customers. As a Cherokee Nation citizen-owned company, we take pride in our work and strive for excellence in everything we do.

With years of experience in computer building and technology, we understand the importance of reliable and efficient systems for gaming, business, office, and school use.
